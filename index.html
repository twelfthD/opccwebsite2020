Revelation of the Hidden-Secret Kingdom of Christ
(the Kingdom that is never to be destroyed)....Daniel 2:44

For the Lord said,

“I will open my mouth in Parables, I will utter hidden things, things from of old—what we have heard and known, what our fathers have told us.

....Psalms 78:2

In the New Testament, at a gathering Jesus spoke on various themes and aspects of the Kingdom of God in parables. He did not say anything without using parables. …So was fulfilled what was spoken through the Prophet:

“I will open my mouth in parables; I will utter things hidden since the creation of the world.” 

....Psalms 78:2

How can anyone enter a strong man’s house and robbed him off his possessions unless he first ties up the strong man?? Only after tying him up can he rob his house. One thing is obvious; the strong man must be tied up first such that he can be robbed. In other words, who is the strong man? In order to find out the will of God it is pertinent to know the name of this strong man. Since he has to be tied up first, it is in the Bible to which and how he is tied up to. Again, since he is to be robbed off his valuables, it is also important to know what valuables he has and where they are located. Therefore, he is tied up and robbed off his belongings and valuables.

....Mathew 12:29

In all there are 120 verses of Parables on the Kingdom of Heaven in the New Testament. By the blessing of Jesus, his apostle Rev. (L) Paolen Guite was blessed with the understanding and interpretation of the Parables of the Teachings of Jesus. He worked tirelessly in spreading the word of God especially his gifted knowledge of understanding on the Parables, God’s Hidden-secret wisdom. He preached in the nook and corner of the state of Manipur, Nagaland, Assam and Mizoram extending up to Myanmar (formerly Burma). Finally, with the guidance and blessings of the Holy Spirit he inaugurated Oneness Pentecostal Christian Church in dedication. This Church is built upon these parables, furthering the blossoming of the Hidden secret-wisdom of the Living scripture about the ‘Kingdom that is never to be destroyed’.

We express our heartfelt gratitude to those engage in spreading the wisdom of God in a mystery, even the hidden wisdom which God ordained before the world unto our glory.And to those who preaches the wisdom of God in a mystery, the Parables, Secret wisdom and Hidden word of God, may it be known that the Word[s] of God should not be [ADDED] nor [TAKEN AWAY] in any form other than the way how it was bestowed to the [Man of God].

May the Holy Spirit of Jesus of Nazareth be upon you and your family and to all who art in the Kingdom of God – the Kingdom that never to be destroyed! Amen...
